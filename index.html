About the Artist

As an artist, my creations are shaped by a lifetime of creative exploration, rooted in world-building, storytelling, and costuming. Growing up, my father immersed me in the limitless realms of table-top RPGs, sparking my fascination of blending creatures with the intricate designs in nature. Through my work, I explore the endless possibilities of form and the untold stories within each handmade piece.

I create with a deep, visceral connection to the worlds and beings I craft. Each piece carries a subtle mystery, inviting viewers to search, infer, and uncover hidden narratives. My creatures strike a balance between imagination and reality, feeling as though they could belong in the depths of nature itself.

The complexity and mystery in my work is meant to spark curiosity and wonder. Each creation is a glimpse into a larger story, offering viewers an invitation to explore an alternate universe where imagination and reality collide, and every creature connects to an unspoken lore.
